The digital call button to convert more customers with voice in your WooCommerce shop. 

== Description ==
SnapCall is a B2B solution for web retailers, put our call button in your WooCommerce store and receive customers calls directly on your smartphone SnapCall app.

✓ **Simplify your customer visitor experience** by allowing customers, in one click and without changing media, to contact the competent person to provide information -> **Increase of the loyalty rate**
✓ **Increase your qualified contacts** focus on the strategic points of the purchase tunnel and prospects with high potential -> **Lower acquisition costs**
✓ **Transform your opportunities into customers** by responding to the users just when they need to conclude a transaction -> **Conversion rate development**

[Try it !](https://admin.snapcall.io/#/inscription)

= Features =
1. unlimited calls
2. set the minimum basket value of the call button display
3. choose pages to display the button
4. define operating hours
5. customer rating
6. follow the conversion of the baskets after a call

== Installation ==
1. Woocommerce have to be installed and functional
2. Install and activate Snapcall plugin
3. Login with your Snapcall account or [register](https://register.snapcall.io) first
4. Create/edit buttons and manage your licences
5. For receiving calls, follow our guide to setup SnapCall app in your [smartphone](https://snapcall.zendesk.com/hc/fr/articles/115005631149-D%C3%A9marrage-rapide-WooCommerce)
